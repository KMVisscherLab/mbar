Resources

This page provides the data dictionary utilized by the McKnight Brain Aging Registry (MBAR). A data dictionary is a comprehensive tool that catalogs and describes the various data elements collected in our studies. It includes definitions, formats, possible values, and the relationships between different data elements, ensuring that all data users have a common understanding of the terms used. The data dictionary is essential for researchers and collaborators to effectively access, interpret, and analyze the data, thereby maintaining the integrity and consistency of the research. Explore this resource to fully leverage the data available for your studies on brain aging and cognitive decline.
